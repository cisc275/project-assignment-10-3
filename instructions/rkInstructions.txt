   RED KNOT INSTRUCTIONS (SIDE-SCROLLING GAME)
   
- Using the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right), control the Red Knot 
  as it migrates south and stops at Delaware along its way to South 
  America.
- It will to eat throughout its flight so collect food to earn points (+1). 
- But WATCH OUT because the Red Knot will encounter different 
  obstacles: planes and buildings.
  (Hitting these means you lose a point (-1))
- The map at the top of the screen shows your progress in migrating to Delaware.
