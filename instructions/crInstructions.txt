   CLAPPER RAIL INSTRUCTIONS (REACTION GAME)

- In this game, you will be trying to get food for the Clapper
  Rail and its young.
- Using the arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right), move in the correct 
  direction to where the food appears
- But WATCH OUT for the prey of the Clapper Rail!
- As you collect food the status bar will increase, but when you hit the 
  prey, it will decrease.
- Fill the status bar to win!
